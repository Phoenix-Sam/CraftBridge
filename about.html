About CraftBridge

Weaving economic empowerment and cultural pride into a single thread.

Our Mission

CraftBridge was born from a simple yet powerful idea: to forge a direct, transparent, and fair connection between the gifted artisans of India and a global community that values heritage and craftsmanship. We saw a system where the creators of immense beauty were often left in the shadows, their profits diminished by intermediaries.

Our mission is to dismantle this unfair structure. By leveraging technology, we empower artisans to take control of their narrative, set their own prices, and earn the livelihood they rightfully deserve. We aim to increase artisan income by 40%, ensuring that their skills are not just preserved, but celebrated and rewarded.

Our Vision

We envision a future where every artisan in India has the digital tools and market access to thrive. Our long-term vision is to digitally preserve over 2,000 unique craft techniques and to become the gold standard for fair trade in the Indian handicraft sector.
